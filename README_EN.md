# Gemini and Google AI Membership Guide

This independent knowledge base explains Google AI plan selection, account ownership, billing channels, payment failures, missing entitlements, renewals and evidence collection.

## Current plan structure

Google currently documents Google AI Plus, Google AI Pro and Google AI Ultra. Availability, storage, features, limits and prices vary by plan and region, so the live Google One or Gemini subscription screen is the source of truth.

## Public service boundary

- ChongGrok only describes assistance for the user's own Google Account.
- Required information is confirmed case by case; no fixed credential is claimed.
- Passwords, two-step verification codes and recovery codes are not requested.
- No ready-made accounts, API credits, SMS verification or bulk registration are offered.
- ChongGrok is not affiliated with or officially partnered with Google.
